About

Hi! I'm Beatriz :)
I’m a philosophy grad student based in Lisbon, working at the intersection of philosophy of mathematics, cognition, and AI. My research focuses mainly on the limits of computation (human or artificial) and on how formal theories of logic and mathematics relate to real cognition.

Since publishing my first paper at 21 (literature is my second passion!) and later joining the same journal as a reviewer, I became especially invested in the art of clear argumentation, careful writing, and collaborative academic work.

This is me when I got into uni! (About 5 years ago hehe)
