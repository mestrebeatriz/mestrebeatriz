Recommendations

Some books I've been reading or recommending for people who want to think about logic, math, and minds.

Absolute Provability and Safe Knowledge of Axioms — Timothy Williamson

Honestly? A great paper...

An Introduction to Gödel's Theorems — Peter Smith

The clearest guide I know to Gödel's incompleteness theorems.

Psychosemantics — Jerry Fodor

An excellent launchpad to computation theories of mind.

Brain Bisection and The Unity of Consciousness — Thomas Nagel

Split-brain cases and what they tell us about the mind.
